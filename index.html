About Us

Our company has been upgrading and modifying cars since 2009. We did over 300 modifications, that involved even a complete chassis reconstruction. We consist of 32 members along which are very experienced workshop owners, rally drivers and car enthusiasists. Main idea originated from Adrian in 2009 when he won a tuning consest earning a whooping $25,000. This earned him enough money to start investing money in a garage, tools and equipment. Vlad and Martin both joined him later that year after travelling to Los Angeles.

After getting several customers very satisfied, we've been showcased in the Muffler tuning magazine. This caused a huge boost in modifications and we began slowly expanding over the Los Angeles. We had to eventually buy another garages and hire more people. As of now, people in Los Angeles know, from where all this tuned cars in town came from. From Car Tuning.
